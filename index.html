Listen up, survivors of MooMochi. This isn't some feel-good bullshit about self-improvement. This is about survival. Our town exists only in our minds and the fragile structures we hastily rebuild every time the Ravagers pass through. Your memory is the only thing standing between us and extinction. Master these techniques or perish. It's that simple.

Core Equations

Memorize these. They're your lifeline when the fastGen construct mookers need blueprints:

1. Ideas per Room (IPR): $$IPR = \frac{I}{R}$$
2. Adjusted Ideas per Room (AIPR): $$AIPR = \frac{I}{R \cdot C}$$
3. Idea Capacity of Room (ICR): $$ICR = k \cdot S$$
4. Idea Saturation Index (ISI): $$ISI = \frac{AIPR}{ICR} \cdot 100\%$$
5. Total Memory Palace Capacity (TMPC): $$TMPC = \sum_{i=1}^{R} ICR_i$$
6. Memory Palace Efficiency (MPE): $$MPE = \frac{I}{TMPC} \cdot 100\%$$

Your salvaged abacus better be calibrated for these equations. One mistake in calculation could mean a collapsed shelter or a breached defense wall.

Critical Locations for Memorization

The Bunker Remnants

Survival classroom in the Bunker Remnants

The Bunker Remnants aren't pretty, but they're all we've got for secure meetings. Memorize every crack, every shadow:

  • Low S value, space is tight
  • High C factor, every detail could mean life or death
  • Limited R, but each room is critical

Calculate the ISI. If it's not above 90%, you're dead weight to the community. Squeeze every bit of information into your mental blueprint.

The Toxic River Junction

Scavengers at the Toxic River Junction

The Junction is our best shot at long-term water filtration. It's a memory nightmare, but essential:

  • Massive S value, the area is vast
  • Extreme C factor, one wrong detail could poison us all
  • High R count, each filtration stage needs perfect recall

Your TMPC calculation here needs to be off the charts. If you can't hold every detail, you'll be on latrine duty until you can.

The Scrapyard Oasis

Hidden garden in the Scrapyard Oasis

Our only source of tech and sustainable food. Memorize it or starve:

  • Variable S, it changes as we salvage and build
  • Moderate C, organized chaos is our survival strategy
  • Fluctuating R, new discoveries change the layout weekly

Focus on MPE here. Efficiency keeps us one step ahead of the Ravagers. Your mind needs to adapt as fast as the Oasis changes.
